# Drawer triage ritual

ID: drawer-triage

## Purpose

Keep deferred work organized without mixing it into active execution.

## Trigger

Run when a new future feature, plan, or non-immediate idea should be recorded.

## Preconditions

- The work is not being executed right now.
- The idea is concrete enough to deserve a written record.

## Steps

1. Capture the work in the drawer surface instead of `desk/`.
1. Keep the description lightweight and future-oriented.
1. Separate speculative backlog items from active implementation tasks.
1. Promote the item into `desk/` once real execution begins.

## Validation

- The work is recorded without polluting the active execution board.
- The promotion rule into `desk/` is explicit.

## Failure Modes

- Treating deferred work as if it were already active.
- Letting speculative backlog items crowd the execution surface.

## Completion

The deferred work is parked cleanly and can later be promoted into `desk/`.
